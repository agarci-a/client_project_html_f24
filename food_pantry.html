Maize and Blue Cupboard

Greater Food Accessbility

Also known as the Pantry, the Maize and Blue Cupboard, offers the community at the University with access to healthy and nourishing food. Whether you are on a financial budget or lack access to transportation to a grocery store, the food pantry is located in the heart of central campus in Betsy Barber to aliviate this need. Even if you feel that you are short on groceries or food, but dont need "help," you are welcome are come in and shop. Appointments are needed to shop at the pantry but there is no limit on the food anyone can take.

Providing Kitchen Items and More

The Food Pantry not only offers food, but also common household and kitchen items. There are food untensils, pots, pans, tupperware, and other kitchenware that the Pantry provides. In addition, it provides common household items such as trash bags, toilet paper, cleaning supplies, and more. However, there is a limited supply of personal, household, and cookware items so you are encouraged to shopping cookware once a semester and personal needs to once a month. Here is a list of the items they provide:

  • School Supplies
  • Hygiene Products
  • Produce
  • Dairy
  • Meat
  • Bread
  • Frozen and Shelf-stable Foods
