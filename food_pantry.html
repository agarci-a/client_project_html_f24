Food Pantry

Greater Food Accessbility

The Food Pantry offers the community at the University with access to healthy and nourishing food. Whether people are on a financial budget or lack access to transportation to a grocery store, the food pantry is located in the heart of central campus by Betsy Barber to aliviate this need.
