Study Spaces

Michigan Union

The Union is one of the most popular study spots for students! Conveniently located on State Street, the Union offers 4 floors of study spaces and nice bathrooms on all floors. In the basement, the floor holds many open study spots and a food court for Panda Express, Subway, and Qdoba. If you are in need to IT services, the Michigan Tech Shop has a location in the basement. On the first floor, the Union is usually busy, offering opening seating outside of the Sweetwaters and Panera Bread if students want a quick bite to

Shapiro Library (Ugli)

The Shapiro Library, or more commonly known as the Ugli, is one of the most popular libraries on central campus. Located at the heart of campus by the Diag, the Ugli has 4 floors of open study spaces, private rooms, computer labs, and a pleathora of charging spots. With a cafe situated at the entrance, students can grab a coffee or a snack when studying. The third floor is newly renovated and offers many comfortable seating. Make sure to come early as the third floor usually gets very busy
