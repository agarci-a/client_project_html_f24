Center for the Education of Women

Empowering Women through Higher Education

The CEW provides resources for women+ and underserved individuals to help them reach their academic, financial, and professional potential. Their services are open to everyone at U-M and in the community who face education and career obstacles. Typical career and educational counseling services for students include discussions around:

  • Navigating the University experience
  • Work/Life balance issues
  • Financial aid options
  • Emergency funding for current U-M Ann Arbor students

Supporting You Reaching Your Potential

The CEW offers a range of services to support the community including events and workshops, funding, counseling, and advocacy initatives. To recieve counseling, you can make an appointment in person or over zoom. During an appointment, you can recieve career counseling, education counseling, or emergency funding information based on your situation.
